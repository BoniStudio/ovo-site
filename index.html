The flow

How OvO works

From a quiet grid to a living object—meaning stays attached every step of the way.

Step 01

Create on a bead grid

Use cyber beads or perler-style grids to design your work with precision and personality.

  • Two grid modes: cyber · perler
  • Palette tuned for bead craft
  • Saved as blueprints you can revisit
Step 02

Give it a meaning

Add a creator note that stays with the piece and makes it feel personal.

  • Up to 150 characters, kept forever
  • Lives on the back of every piece
Step 03

Turn it into a real piece

Minting moves your work from draft to a collectible object in your archive.

  • Numbered and dated
  • Tied to creator and note
Step 04

Send it forward

Gift it to someone else while keeping the creator, the meaning, and the journey attached.

  • Transfer with full provenance
  • Story stays, even as it moves

Questions

FAQ

What is OvO?

OvO is a bead art app where you can create, mint, collect, display, and gift digital bead works.

Does the note stay with the piece?

Yes. The creator note is designed to stay with the piece as part of its identity.

Can I gift my work to someone else?

Yes. Gifting is a core part of OvO. The work can move, while its story stays attached.

What do I get with Pro?

Pro gives you larger canvases, special effect beads, stronger collection tools, and more creative freedom.
