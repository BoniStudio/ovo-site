FAQ

What is OvO?

OvO is a bead art app where you can create, mint, collect, display, and gift digital bead works.

Does the note stay with the piece?

Yes. The creator note is designed to stay with the piece as part of its identity.

Can I gift my work to someone else?

Yes. Gifting is a core part of OvO. The work can move, while its story stays attached.

What do I get with Pro?

Pro gives you larger canvases, special effect beads, stronger collection tools, and more creative freedom.
